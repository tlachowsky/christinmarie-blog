Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: May 2026

Hi friend — let me be straightforward with you about how this site works behind the scenes.

Affiliate Links

Some of the posts on this site contain affiliate links to products I genuinely use and recommend. If you click one of those links and make a purchase, I may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you.

I want to be very clear: I only link to products I have personally used and would recommend to a friend in real life. I am not interested in cluttering this site with things I do not believe in.

Sponsored Content

If a post is ever sponsored by a brand or partner, I will say so clearly at the top of the post. Sponsorships do not change my honest opinion of the products. If something is not good, I am not going to tell you it is good.

Why I Do This

Affiliate income helps keep this little corner of the internet running — the hosting, the domain, the time it takes to write. Every click and purchase through one of my links genuinely helps, and I appreciate it more than I can say.

Questions

If you ever have a question about a recommendation or want more information about a product I have shared, you can always reach me at hello@christinmarie.us. I will write back.

Thank you for being here. ♡
